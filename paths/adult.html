WHO THIS PATH IS FOR

The Adult Path is built for anyone 18+ who wants a structured, coached training experience. Whether you are returning to fitness after years away, pushing toward a new milestone, or looking for accountability that actually works — this is your entry point.

Every class is coached. Every session has structure. Every member has access to open gym hours, locker rooms, showers, and towel service. This is not a place where you swipe a card and figure it out alone.
