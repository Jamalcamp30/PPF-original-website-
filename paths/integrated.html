INTEGRATED
PATH

For individuals with unique needs and their families. Dignity-centered, adaptive coaching with full transparency, open communication, and measurable progress.

WHO THIS PATH IS FOR

The Integrated Fitness path is built for individuals of all ages with adaptive needs — including developmental, physical, and neurological differences. This is not a side program or an afterthought. It is a fully designed coaching system built on dignity, structure, communication, and measurable progress.

Every session is supervised, sensory-aware, and designed around the individual. Families are welcome to observe, participate, and stay involved throughout the process.

HOW ENROLLMENT WORKS

Step 1 — Initial Consultation: A phone or in-person conversation with Rebecca to understand the individual's needs, goals, comfort level, and family expectations.

Step 2 — Intake Assessment: A coach-led, sensory-aware assessment of movement ability, comfort, and communication style. Parents welcome to observe.

Step 3 — Program Design: A personalized plan is built — adapted class sessions, 1:1 coaching, or day program support. Scheduling around the family.

Step 4 — First Session: A guided, low-pressure session designed to build trust and establish comfort.

WHAT TO EXPECT OVER TIME

Week 1: Comfort building. Routine introduction. Coach-family debrief after every session. Establishing trust and familiarity.

Week 4: Growing independence. Recognized routines. First milestone review with the family. Adjustments based on observed progress.

Month 3: Visible confidence gains. Task independence emerging. Full progress report shared with the family. Long-term plan established.
