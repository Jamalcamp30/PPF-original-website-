BUILT FOR THE ATHLETE
YOUR CHILD IS BECOMING.

North Atlanta's standard for serious young athletes. Sport-specific training, measurable results, and coaches who know the difference between tired and improving — for middle school, high school, and college-bound athletes across Milton, Roswell, Alpharetta, Johns Creek, Cumming, and Forsyth.

WHO THIS PATH IS FOR

The Athlete Path is built for the kid whose talent deserves a real program — not a workout. Whether your athlete is preparing for a combine, training around a sport season, or building a four-year foundation before college, every session is assessed, measured, and adjusted — not generic.

We train the athlete, not the age group. Every kid who walks in gets a baseline, a file, and a four-week re-test. Force plates, film, and periodization — coached at the same standard your child will face at the next level, taught earlier, taught right.

Most gyms run classes. We run programs. You'll see the numbers change — in writing, every four weeks.

WHAT YOU GET

Combine Prep: Sprint mechanics, 40-yard development, pro agility, vertical and broad jump coaching with timing gate accuracy.

Speed Systems: Acceleration drills, top-speed mechanics, first-step explosion, and change-of-direction protocols.

Strength Development: Periodized lifting programs built around your sport, position, and development timeline.

Metrics Tracking: Every benchmark test is recorded. Every PR is documented. You will see the numbers change.

Coach Film Review: Movement is analyzed like film. Your coach will show you what they see, what to correct, and what the fix looks like in practice.
