ATHLETE
PATH

For competitive athletes ages 12+ who want measurable speed, strength, and performance development from real coaching — not just workouts.

WHO THIS PATH IS FOR

The Athlete Path is built for youth and high-school athletes who are serious about performance. Whether you are preparing for a combine, training for a specific sport, or building a competitive foundation, this path is structured around measurable development, coached sessions, and real accountability.

Every session is periodized, filmed-level coached, and connected to a benchmark system that tracks your improvement over time. You do not just train here — you develop.

WHAT YOU GET

Combine Prep: Sprint mechanics, 40-yard development, pro agility, vertical and broad jump coaching with timing gate accuracy.

Speed Systems: Acceleration drills, top-speed mechanics, first-step explosion, and change-of-direction protocols.

Strength Development: Periodized lifting programs built around your sport, position, and development timeline.

Metrics Tracking: Every benchmark test is recorded. Every PR is documented. You will see the numbers change.

Coach Film Review: Movement is analyzed like film. Your coach will show you what they see, what to correct, and what the fix looks like in practice.
