# PPF Athletics Style Bible

## Creative thesis

PPF should feel like a serious performance institution with a human center—not a generic gym, a luxury wellness club, or a collection of landing-page components. The site should make coaching, standards, measurable outcomes, and belonging feel inseparable.

## Reference synthesis

The art direction combines three useful reference qualities without copying their layouts:

- **EXOS:** performance science, clear positioning, evidence before decoration.
- **DOGPOUND:** industrial restraint, image-first storytelling, physical intensity.
- **Equinox:** controlled pacing, premium typography, decisive calls to action.

## Mood

Disciplined. Cinematic. Earned. Precise. Inclusive without becoming soft. Premium without becoming ornamental.

## Color

- Near-black foundation: `#080808`
- Warm off-white evidence sections: `#F1EEE7`
- PPF orange action color: `#FF4F00`
- Pure white for high-contrast type
- No stock gradients. Gradients may only solve legibility or spatial transition problems.

## Typography

- **Display:** Bebas Neue, oversized and tightly paced, for declarations and numbers.
- **Body/UI:** Inter, restrained and readable, for detail and decisions.
- Outlined display type is used sparingly to create depth and hierarchy—not as decoration on every heading.

## Motion

Motion carries the story instead of decorating it.

- The hero moves between two frames from the same performance world.
- Scale, crossfade, and scroll reveals should feel physical and controlled.
- No constant floating, glowing, spinning, or ornamental particle effects.
- Reduced-motion preferences must be respected.

## Pacing

- The hero establishes the standard immediately.
- Each training path becomes a full chapter, not a card in a grid.
- Integrated Fitness receives a distinct mission moment.
- Proof reads like a performance ledger.
- Leadership reads like an editorial feature.
- The conversion action remains one glance away.

## House rules

1. No template hero followed by three cards.
2. No stock gradients.
3. No effect without a narrative or usability purpose.
4. Every section must earn its scroll through new information, emotion, or proof.
5. Full-height sections are used only where they strengthen pacing; content is never stretched merely to satisfy a viewport rule.
6. PPF photography and verified results outrank generic stock assets.
7. Mobile is a distinct composition, not a squeezed desktop layout.
8. The 3-Day Experience remains continuously accessible without blocking content.

## Quality bar

A working designer should not be able to identify the homepage as an off-the-shelf AI template. The page must have a coherent visual thesis, intentional pacing, responsive behavior, accessible motion, and a clear conversion path.
