FAMILY PREP CENTER

Preparing for the Integrated Path

A resource center for families entering the Integrated Path.

🏠

What to Expect on Day One

The facility is spacious with controlled lighting and manageable sound levels. Equipment is clearly organized and age-appropriate. Richard or Rebecca will greet you at the door, walk you through the space, and introduce the routine step by step — no surprises, no pressure.

👨‍👩‍👧

How Parents Can Prepare

Dress your child in comfortable athletic clothing and supportive shoes. Arrive 10 minutes early so there's no rush. Let your child explore the space at their own pace. Stay calm and positive — your energy sets the tone. Parents are welcome to observe the full session.

📈

Progress Timeline

Month 1: Building trust, learning the routine, first movement patterns. Month 3: Increased confidence, independently following cues, measurable strength gains. Month 6: Ownership of the routine, consistent performance benchmarks, social engagement with peers.

💬

Communication & Updates

After every session, your coach provides direct feedback — what went well and what to work on. Monthly progress reports track strength, movement quality, and engagement. Parent check-ins are scheduled regularly so you always know where your child stands.
