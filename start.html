INTEGRATED ENROLLMENT

Getting Started Is
Clear and Simple.

Here is exactly what happens when a family reaches out about the Integrated Fitness path — from initial contact to long-term growth.

01
Initial Consultation
A phone or in-person conversation with Rebecca to understand the individual's needs, goals, comfort level, and family expectations. No forms. No pressure. Just a real conversation.
02
Intake Assessment
A coach-led, sensory-aware assessment of movement ability, comfort, and communication style. Parents are welcome to observe and participate in the process.
03
Program Design
A personalized plan is built around the individual — adapted class sessions, 1:1 coaching, or day program support. Scheduling is designed around the family's availability.
04
First Session
The individual begins with a guided, low-pressure session designed to build trust, establish comfort, and set the foundation for consistent participation.
WHAT TO EXPECT OVER TIME
WEEK 1
Comfort building. Routine introduction. Coach-family debrief after every session. Establishing trust and familiarity with the environment.
WEEK 4
Growing independence. Recognized routines. First milestone review with the family. Adjustments made based on observed progress and comfort level.
MONTH 3
Visible confidence gains. Task independence emerging. Full progress report shared with the family. Long-term development plan established.

PRICING: Integrated sessions are structured around individual needs. Contact Rebecca directly for a personalized consultation and pricing.
