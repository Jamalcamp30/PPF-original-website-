QUESTIONS FROM JOHNS CREEK ATHLETES

How far is PPF from Johns Creek?
PPF Athletics is roughly 20 minutes from central Johns Creek via GA-400 North. Exit at Majors Rd (Exit 14) and you'll be at our facility in under a mile. Many Johns Creek families pair the drive with after-school errands and make it part of their routine.
What does speed training at PPF actually include?
Our speed program covers sprint mechanics, acceleration technique, lateral quickness, and top-end speed development. Every session is coached — not just supervised — with video analysis available for athletes preparing for showcases, combines, and tryouts.
Can my child train here if they play multiple sports?
Absolutely — multi-sport athletes are our specialty. We build general athletic qualities (speed, power, agility, durability) that transfer across football, soccer, baseball, lacrosse, basketball, and more. Programming adjusts based on which season your athlete is in.
Do you offer any trial sessions for new clients?
Yes. Every new athlete or adult starts with a complimentary 3-day experience. You'll go through an initial assessment, train with our coaches, and get a taste of PPF culture before making any commitment. No hard sells — just quality coaching.
What sets PPF apart from other training facilities near Johns Creek?
Three things: coaching depth (20+ years of combined experience, not fresh certifications), individualized programming (your plan is yours, not a group template), and accountability (we track progress and expect results, not just attendance). That combination is hard to find anywhere in North Metro Atlanta.
