QUESTIONS FROM CUMMING RESIDENTS

Where exactly is PPF Athletics located in Cumming?
We're at 7721 Majors Rd, Cumming, GA 30041 — right in the heart of Cumming. Most residents reach us in under 10 minutes, with easy access from GA-400 and Bethelview Road. Plenty of free parking on-site.
Is there free parking at the facility?
Yes! We have a dedicated parking lot with plenty of space for athletes and families. You'll never have to worry about finding a spot — even during our busiest after-school hours.
What ages do you train?
We work with athletes as young as 10 and adults of all ages. Our youth programs are built for middle-school and high-school athletes, and our adult path serves everyone from weekend warriors to former college athletes looking to stay in peak shape.
Can I try a session before committing?
Absolutely. We offer a complimentary 3-day experience so you can see our facility, meet our coaches, and feel the difference of real performance training. No contracts, no pressure — just results.
Do you offer combine and showcase preparation for high school athletes?
Yes — combine prep is one of our specialties. We coach athletes through 40-yard dash technique, pro agility, vertical jump, and sport-specific testing so they show up prepared and confident for showcases and college recruiting events.
