QUESTIONS FROM ALPHARETTA FAMILIES

How far is PPF Athletics from Alpharetta?
We're about 15 minutes north of downtown Alpharetta via GA-400. Take Exit 14 (Majors Rd) and you're practically at our door. Many of our clients from Alpharetta say the drive is easier than navigating traffic to gyms along North Point Parkway.
Why should I drive to Cumming instead of training locally?
The coaching standard at PPF Athletics is hard to replicate in a standard gym. We provide individualized programming, certified performance coaches with 20+ years of experience, and a facility designed specifically for athletic training — not treadmill rows and cable machines. Fifteen minutes of driving buys you a dramatically different training experience.
Do you train high-school athletes from Alpharetta schools?
Absolutely. We regularly work with athletes from Alpharetta High, Milton High, and other North Fulton schools. Our programs are designed around school sports calendars so we can build strength and speed during off-season and maintain it in-season.
What youth programs do you offer?
Our youth athlete program covers ages 10 and up. We focus on foundational movement skills, speed mechanics, and sport-specific conditioning. Every athlete gets an initial assessment and a custom plan tailored to their sport, position, and goals.
