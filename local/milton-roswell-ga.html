QUESTIONS FROM MILTON & ROSWELL

How far is PPF from Milton and Roswell?
From Milton, we're approximately 12–15 minutes north via GA-400 or Bethany Bend to Majors Rd. From Roswell, expect about 18–20 minutes. Both routes are straightforward with minimal turns once you're off 400.
Can my whole family train at PPF?
That's exactly what we're built for. Our Integrated Path lets parents and athletes train simultaneously. You'll each have your own coach and program, but you arrive and leave together. It's the most efficient use of your family's time.
What makes PPF different from personal training at my local gym?
Most commercial gyms offer generalist trainers working off templates. At PPF, every program is individualized and coached by specialists with deep experience in athletic performance and adult fitness. We also have purpose-built training space — turf, racks, sleds, and more — that a standard gym floor can't match.
Is there convenient parking for families?
Yes — our facility has a spacious, free parking lot. Even during peak hours after school, there's always room. You won't be circling a garage or walking across a shopping center.
Do you have evening and weekend availability?
Absolutely. We know Milton and Roswell families have packed schedules, so we offer after-school, evening, and Saturday session times. Contact us to find a slot that works for your household.
