QUESTIONS FROM FORSYTH COUNTY PARENTS

What ages do you train at PPF Athletics?
Our youth athlete programs start at age 10 and extend through high school. We also train college-age athletes during breaks and adults of all ages. Every program is tailored to the individual's developmental stage and goals.
Where in Forsyth County are you located?
We're at 7721 Majors Rd in Cumming — centrally positioned in Forsyth County. Whether you're coming from the GA-400 corridor, Castleberry Road, or Buford Highway side of the county, most families reach us in 10–15 minutes.
How do I schedule sessions around my child's school and sports calendar?
We offer flexible scheduling with after-school, evening, and Saturday sessions. Our coaches also adjust training intensity and focus based on whether your athlete is in-season, off-season, or in a tournament cycle. We work with your calendar, not against it.
Do you work with local Forsyth County school teams?
We train individual athletes from many Forsyth County schools including South Forsyth, West Forsyth, Lambert, Denmark, Forsyth Central, and North Forsyth. While we're not directly affiliated with school programs, our athletes regularly credit PPF training for their on-field improvements.
Is there a trial period for new families?
Yes — every family starts with a complimentary 3-day experience. Your athlete (and you, if you'd like) will go through an assessment, train with our coaches, and experience our facility before making any financial commitment. It's the best way to see if PPF is the right fit.
