Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

PPF Athletics (“PPF,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of every individual who engages with our website, services, and communications.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal information submitted through our website, inquiry forms, consultations, memberships, and related interactions.

01

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including your name, email address, phone number, and any additional information you submit through contact forms, consultation requests, trial applications, membership inquiries, or other website submissions.

We may also collect limited technical and usage information through standard website technologies, including browser type, device information, IP address, referral source, and general site activity. This information helps us maintain, improve, and secure our website and operations.

02

How We Use Information

Information collected through our website may be used to:

  • respond to inquiries and consultation requests;
  • communicate regarding services, scheduling, memberships, and support;
  • manage internal business operations and client relationships;
  • improve website functionality and user experience; and
  • satisfy legal, regulatory, and administrative obligations.
03

Communications

By submitting your information through our website, you consent to being contacted by PPF Athletics by phone, email, or text message regarding your inquiry, requested services, membership interest, or related business matters.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use submitted information for unrelated solicitation.

04

Disclosure of Information

We may disclose information only where reasonably necessary, including to trusted service providers who support our website, communications, scheduling, or payment systems, or where disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect the rights, safety, or operations of PPF Athletics.

05

Data Protection

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect the information entrusted to us. While we are committed to responsible data handling, no transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be fully secure, and absolute security cannot be assured.

06

Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill legitimate business purposes, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies and agreements.

07

Minors

Certain PPF services are designed for youth athletes. When information is submitted on behalf of a minor, we expect that submission to be made by a parent or legal guardian, or with appropriate parental or guardian authorization.

08

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to or integrations with third-party platforms, including scheduling, payment, or social media services. PPF Athletics is not responsible for the privacy or data practices of those third parties.

09

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
