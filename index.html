THE STANDARD INDEX

We Don't Just Count Reps.
We Measure What Matters.

These are the six dimensions PPF holds every member accountable to. Not vanity metrics. Not before-and-after photos. The real indicators of coached progression.

01
CONSISTENCY
Show up. Not when it's convenient — when it's scheduled. The foundation of everything PPF builds is built on presence.
02
COACHING RESPONSE
How quickly a member applies a coaching cue. Not compliance — internalization. The cue becomes the habit.
03
MOVEMENT QUALITY
Can you move correctly under load, under fatigue, under pressure? That's what separates trained from untrained.
04
OUTPUT
What you produce when it counts — watts, velocity, load, time. Measurable, repeatable, coachable.
05
ACCOUNTABILITY
Do you own your training? Logging, tracking, communicating, adjusting. The system works when you work with it.
06
REPEATABILITY
Can you reproduce performance on demand? One good day means nothing. Consistent excellence under varying conditions — that is the standard.
