QUESTIONS

Before You
Walk In.

Answers to the questions people actually ask before they commit.

Who is PPF for?
Competitive athletes (ages 12–22), adults of any fitness level, and individuals with adaptive needs. Three paths, one coaching standard. You do not need to be in shape to start.
What ages do you train?
Athletes as young as 12. Adults of all ages. Integrated path members of all ages. There is no upper limit — only a coaching standard that adapts to where you are.
How does the 3-Day Experience work?
You sign up, come in for three coached sessions at no cost, and train alongside current members. Day 1 is about 60 minutes: assessment, orientation, and your first coached session. After the three days, you decide if PPF is the right fit — no pressure, no contract lock-in.
What happens after the 3-Day Experience?
If you decide to join, you pick the membership that fits your path. Monthly group training is $159/month. Private coaching is sold in 10-session bundles. You’ll be coached from day one — no figuring-it-out period.
How is adaptive / integrated training handled?
Rebecca built the Integrated Path from the ground up, drawing on 18 years of special education experience. Sessions are individually designed around the member’s abilities, coached with the same rigor as every other path, and focused on building real independence over time.
How often should I train?
Most athletes train 3–5 days per week. Adults typically train 2–4 days. Integrated members train 1–3 days depending on their program. Your coach will help you find the right cadence.
I’m an adult and new to training. Is this beginner-friendly?
Yes. The Adult Performance path is built for all levels — including people who have never touched a barbell. Every session is coached, every movement is scaled to your ability, and your program progresses as you do. You will never be left to figure it out alone.
What should I bring to Day 1?
Athletic clothes, court or training shoes (no sandals), and a water bottle. We provide everything else. Arrive 10 minutes early so we can walk you through the space before your session starts.
